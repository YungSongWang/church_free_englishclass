OUR ENGLISH CLASSES

Learn English in Supportive Environment

Our English class is designed for beginner to intermediate learners. We focus on practical, everyday English to help you communicate confidently in various situations.

The class size is kept small to ensure personalized attention for each student.

We use an interactive and engaging teaching method that encourages active participation. Our lessons include conversation practice, vocabulary building, and basic grammar.

We also incorporate fun activities and real-life scenarios to make learning both enjoyable and effective.

We offer two weekly sessions to accommodate different schedules:

  • Wednesday evenings: 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM
  • Saturday mornings: 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM

    • Classes are held at 195 North Terrace, Adelaide SA 5000. New students are welcome to join at any time - attend one or both sessions weekly as suits your schedule.

    Course Features

    Small Class Sizes

    We offer small class sizes to ensure each student receives personalized attention and guidance.

    Interactive Lessons

    Our classes are highly interactive, with group discussions, conversation practice, and role-playing exercises to improve speaking skills.

    Experienced Instructors

    Our teaching team has years of experience, helping students to quickly grasp language skills.

    Practical Materials

    We provide practical materials that are closely related to everyday situations, helping students apply what they learn in real life.

    Free Learning Materials

    We provide all the necessary learning materials for free, ensuring that students have the resources they need to succeed.

    Culturally Diverse Teaching Staff

    Our instructors come from diverse cultural backgrounds and can sometimes even help you understand concepts using your native language, enhancing your learning experience.
